About Us

Welcome to amidependent.com! Our mission is to help students and families understand the complex rules of financial aid dependency status.

We provide clear, accessible tools and information to help you determine if you are considered an independent student for FAFSA and other financial aid programs.

We believe that with the right information, students can confidently navigate their financial aid journey and secure the funding they deserve.

If you have questions or want to learn more, feel free to reach out on our Contact page.
